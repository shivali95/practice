📖 Kedarnath Katha
Praachin gatha
Pandavon ne Shiva ko yahan paaya
Kurukshetra yudh ke baad Pandav apne paap ke liye Shiv ji ka prayaschit chahte the. Shiva unse bachne ke liye nandi (bail) ka rup le kar Himalaya aaye. Pandavon ne pehchaan liya · Shiv ne dharti mein ghus ke bhaga. Keval peeth se bail ka kukudh Kedarnath mein prakat hua. Yahi wajah hai ki yahan Shiv-lingam nahi, balki kukudh-ake roop ki puja hoti hai.
